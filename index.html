HerTechdail Academy is the training arm of HerTechTrail, a social-impact organization focused on empowering African women with in-demand tech and digital skills. It offers fully remote, flexible, beginner-friendly courses in areas like UI/UX design, web development, product design, data analytics, product management, and digital marketing. Learners gain hands-on project experience, mentorship, community support, and certificates upon completion, all while being part of a global network of women in tech. The academy’s mission is to close the gender gap in the tech industry by helping women build career-ready skills and access opportunities.
